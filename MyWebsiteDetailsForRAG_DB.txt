## PART 2: FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)

### GENERAL & PRODUCT QUESTIONS

#### Q: What exactly is an Autonomous AI Agent?
**A:** Unlike traditional rigid chatbots that only follow pre-written if/then scripts, NexusAI Autonomous Agents are powered by advanced Large Language Models (LLMs) featuring advanced reasoning and tool-use capabilities. They can break down abstract, complex corporate tasks, self-correct if an intermediate step fails, and interact natively with your third-party business software to complete end-to-end workflows independently.

#### Q: Which third-party platforms can these agents integrate with?
**A:** Our agents are built to live exactly where your business operates. They connect natively via secure API accesses to standard corporate tech stacks including Slack, Salesforce, HubSpot, GitHub, Microsoft Teams, Jira, PostgreSQL, and thousands of other applications through custom webhooks.

#### Q: Can I run multiple types of agents simultaneously in my company?
**A:** Absolutely. You can build an interconnected digital workforce. For example, your **Support Agent** can receive an escalated complex technical ticket, hand off the raw systemic data to an **Analyst Agent** to query your private database, and pass the final insights back to your human supervisors for review.
